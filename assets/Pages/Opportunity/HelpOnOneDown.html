What Is Help On One Down ?

"Help On One Down" is an initiative focused on creating a nurturing ecosystem for underprivileged children in Nepal. It aims to shape their emotional, intellectual, and social development to inspire future leaders and achievers. The project emphasizes personal growth and empowerment, aspiring to foster the next generation of transformational figures like Ratan Tata, but with roots in Nepal.

Ecosystem for Shaping Future Leaders of Nepal: One Child, One Smile at A time


Mission Statement

"With 'Help On One Down,' our mission is to create an ecosystem that supports and guides underprivileged children in Nepal. By nurturing their emotional growth, personality, and aspirations, we aim to pave the way for the next great visionaries, like Ratan Tata or other inspiring leaders, to emerge from our nation."


Our Goals

  • To build a supportive community dedicated to helping children realize their full potential.
  • To provide resources, mentorship, and branding support for underprivileged children, ensuring they grow into emotionally resilient and socially impactful individuals.
  • To establish programs that create sustainable, long-term change by addressing emotional well-being, education, and personal development.

Requirements for Joining the Ecosystem

If you wish to be part of our mission, here's what we look for:


  • A commitment to creating positive change in the lives of underprivileged children.
  • An innovative mindset and the drive to serve the nation.
  • A passion for making a tangible difference in our beautiful nation.
  • Organizations or individuals who align with our mission of long-term impact over short-term benefits.

Application Process

To join the program, follow these steps:


  • Fill out our online application form detailing your interest and how you plan to contribute.
  • Based on your application, shortlisted candidates will be invited for a discussion creating impact.
  • Final selection will focus on passion, feasibility of ideas, and alignment with our mission.
