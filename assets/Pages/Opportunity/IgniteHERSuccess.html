Women's Business Incubator for Entrepreneurial Innovation In Nepal

Our Goals

The goal is to establish a Women's Business Incubator that provides an environment for motivated women entrepreneurs to collaborate and innovate to develop and launch their own businesses. The incubator aims to support women interested in creating new employment opportunities for themselves and others.

Requirements for Joining:

To join our network, you must meet the following requirements:


  • Must be a woman aged 22 or older.
  • If you don’t meet our age mark and still have an innovative idea.
  • Must have a business idea or an existing business in need of growth and development.
  • The business idea must be feasible, scalable, and have the potential to create social impact.
  • Applicants must submit a business proposal outlining their idea or existing business and how they plan to develop it.
  • The company will review the proposals and select the most promising candidates.

Application Process

To apply to join our network, please follow these steps:.


  • Interested candidates can apply by filling out an online application form.
  • Candidates will be selected based on the strength of their business ideas, motivation, and commitment to the program.
  • Shortlisted candidates will be invited for a meeting to discuss further regularities.
  • Final selection will be made based on the interview and overall fit with the program.

Once we have received your application, we will review your portfolio or resume to ensure that you meet our requirements. If you meet our requirements, we will contact you to discuss next steps.


Incubator Program:

We're looking for freelancers who have the following skills and experience:


  • The program will be divided into multiple stages, each with a specific focus on business planning, strategy, marketing, financial management, and legal and regulatory issues.
  • Participants will receive mentorship, training, and support to develop their business ideas.
  • Regular networking events and workshops will be conducted to facilitate knowledge sharing and collaboration among participants.
  • The incubator will provide access to resources such as office space, equipment, and funding opportunities.
  • The program will run for a set period of time, typically 6-12 months, during which participants will work on developing their businesses and achieving specific milestones.

We're looking for freelancers who have the following skills and experience:


  • Strength of the business idea and potential for growth and success.

  • The social impact that the business idea can create.
  • The applicant's commitment and passion for the business idea.
